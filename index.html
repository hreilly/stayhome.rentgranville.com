More than 90% of apartment searches already start online, so why not make the rest of the process accessible from anywhere? When it comes down to it, it's Granville's job to meet these changing needs.

What We're Doing

At Granville Realty, we have always believed in the power of community. We know we must all work together in the face of uncertainty, and there is no higher priority to us than the wellbeing of our current & prospective tenants and our staff. That's why we're striving to create systems that still provide services to you while protecting one another and minimizing in-person interaction. Wherever possible, our staff are working from home so they can be with their families and we can all do our part to keep each other safe. We hope that you won't hesitate to reach out to us with any question or concerns. Below, you'll find helpful information about how we're handling these temporary changes.

Virtual Appointments & Tours

We know life doesn't stop, even when the world is facing a global health crisis. A safe, comfortable place to live is still a basic necessity, and we are committed to providing housing, no matter what difficulties we're all facing. That's why GV Realty is providing alternative virtual appointments with our leasing agents as you search for your next apartment, home rental, or commercial space. They'll be available to answer all your questions and even give you video tours of available units. Just send us an email or call one of our leasing offices to schedule an appointment.

A Message to Our Tenants:

In order to reduce exposure for our technicians, leasing agents, and your families, we will be rescheduling and/or delaying some non-emergency repair work orders until public officials have lifted the recommended social distancing and self-isolation guidelines. In addition, the following policies will be in place until recommendations from health and governmental organizations change:

  • Outdoor spaces are still open, but we encourage you to practice the 6-foot social distancing rule recommended by the CDC.
  • All indoor shared common spaces are closed until further notice (gyms, clubhouses, etc.). Laundry rooms will remain open, but we encourage you to practice social distancing and sanitize surfaces before & after use.
  • In-person community events are cancelled until further notice.
  • We are encouraging everyone to make rent payments using our online payment portal. If you need to set up an online account, please contact your property manager.
  • If you are facing financial difficulties related to COVID-19, please reach out to your property manager as soon as possible. We're here to help.

We truly appreciate your patience and consideration as we all work together to protect our communities and our loved ones. Our whole team sincerely thanks you for helping us help each other.

Our office will still be open by appointment for new move-ins and for our valued vendors & partners. If you need to schedule an appointment, please contact the appropriate leasing office. Policies are subject to change at any time. We will be updating our operating procedures based on current recommendations from health and governmental organizations. Thank you for your patience and consideration during this time.
